Resources

* Python

1. https://leetcode.com/problemset/all/ and https://leetcode.com/explore/ - No better way to practice than to practice!
2. https://docs.python-guide.org/intro/learning/ - Extensive set of learning resources

* Linear algebra and Statistics

1. https://www.khanacademy.org/math/linear-algebra - great videos on linear algebra. Not everything is relevant to data science.
2. http://faculty.marshall.usc.edu/gareth-james/ISL/ - excellent book to dive into the details of statistics and ML algorithms
3. https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability - great courses on statistics. Many of these are relevant to data science.
